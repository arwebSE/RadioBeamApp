Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Overview

RadioBeam is an iOS radio app for discovering and streaming live radio stations. The app does not require an account and does not run a RadioBeam-owned user tracking backend.

RadioBeam uses third-party services for station discovery, radio streaming, artwork lookup, advertising, diagnostics, and app performance/usage measurement.

Information Stored on Your Device

RadioBeam stores app preferences locally on your device, including favorite stations, starred countries, playback-related settings, and diagnostic preferences. This data is used to make the app work and is not sold.

Search and Browsing Activity

When you search or browse for stations, RadioBeam may use that activity to provide app functionality, such as filtering station lists and requesting station data from radio directory services. RadioBeam does not require an account for this.

Location

RadioBeam may use approximate location or country-related information to support station discovery and app functionality. Third-party services used by the app, including advertising or analytics providers, may also process coarse location or device-related information according to their own privacy practices and your device settings. RadioBeam does not use precise location to track you across apps or websites.

Radio Streaming and Metadata

When you play a station, RadioBeam connects to the station stream URL and related station services. Station providers may receive technical connection information such as your IP address, device network information, and playback request details according to their own practices.

RadioBeam uses the public Radio Browser API to discover stations and may notify Radio Browser when a station is played so station popularity and availability data can be maintained.

Artwork Lookup

When stream metadata includes a likely song and artist, RadioBeam may query Apple's iTunes Search API to find matching artwork. The query can include song and artist text provided by the radio stream.

Advertising

RadioBeam may show limited, non-interrupting banner ads in the iPhone app through Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may process data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, coarse location, product interaction, advertising data, diagnostics, and performance data where permitted by your device settings, consent choices, and applicable law. RadioBeam does not place full-screen ads, interstitial ads, forced ad flows, or ads in the CarPlay listening flow.

Your Choices

  • You can remove favorites and starred countries inside the app.
  • You can manage location permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage Apple advertising and tracking controls in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage tracking permission, advertising identifier access, and personalized advertising controls in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored RadioBeam data from the device.

Children

RadioBeam is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app provides access to live radio streams, and station content is controlled by the station providers.

Changes

This policy may be updated as RadioBeam changes. The updated version will be published at this URL with a new updated date.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the RadioBeam support page.
